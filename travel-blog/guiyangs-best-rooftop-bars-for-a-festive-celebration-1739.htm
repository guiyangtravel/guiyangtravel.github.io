Guiyang’s Best Rooftop Bars for a Festive Celebration

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the vibrant capital of Guizhou Province, is a city where modernity meets tradition. Known for its lush landscapes, spicy cuisine, and rich ethnic culture, it’s also home to a thriving nightlife scene. For travelers looking to celebrate in style, rooftop bars offer the perfect blend of stunning views, creative cocktails, and a festive atmosphere. Here’s a curated list of Guiyang’s best rooftop bars to elevate your celebration.

The Skyline Lounge: Panoramic Views & Craft Cocktails

Perched atop one of Guiyang’s tallest buildings, The Skyline Lounge is a must-visit for anyone seeking a luxurious rooftop experience.

Why It Stands Out

  • 360-Degree Views: Overlook the city’s skyline, with the Nanming River glistening below.
  • Signature Drinks: Try their Moutai Mojito, a local twist on the classic cocktail featuring Guizhou’s famous liquor.
  • Live Music: Weekend DJ sets and live bands add to the vibrant ambiance.

Pro Tip

Arrive just before sunset to witness the city transition from day to night—a truly magical moment.

Cloud Nine: A Bohemian Rooftop Escape

For a more laid-back yet stylish vibe, Cloud Nine combines bohemian decor with a cozy atmosphere.

Highlights

  • Eclectic Decor: Think hanging lanterns, lush greenery, and mismatched vintage furniture.
  • Local Flavors: Their Spicy Mango Margarita incorporates Guizhou’s beloved chili-infused flavors.
  • Cultural Nights: Monthly events feature local musicians and artists, making it a hub for creatives.

Best For

Travelers who want a relaxed setting with a touch of Guiyang’s artistic spirit.

The Zenith: Luxury Meets Tradition

If you’re celebrating a special occasion, The Zenith offers an upscale rooftop experience with a nod to Guizhou’s heritage.

What to Expect

  • Elegant Ambiance: Sleek design with traditional Miao embroidery accents.
  • Gourmet Bites: Pair your drinks with tapas-style dishes inspired by Guizhou cuisine.
  • Private Cabins: Book a secluded pod for an intimate gathering.

Don’t Miss

Their Baijiu Old Fashioned, a sophisticated take on China’s national spirit.

Starlight Terrace: A Hidden Gem

Tucked away in the heart of the city, Starlight Terrace is a lesser-known spot with a charming, intimate feel.

Why Locals Love It

  • Cozy Atmosphere: Fairy lights and wooden decor create a warm, inviting space.
  • Affordable Luxury: High-quality cocktails at reasonable prices.
  • Seasonal Menus: Their drinks rotate with local ingredients, like summer lychee or winter persimmon.

Insider’s Pick

Ask for the Midnight in Guiyang, a smoky, herbal cocktail exclusive to this bar.

Horizon Bar: Modern Minimalism

For those who prefer a sleek, contemporary vibe, Horizon Bar delivers with its minimalist design and innovative mixology.

Standout Features

  • Infinity Edge Pool: A rare feature for rooftop bars in Guiyang.
  • Mixology Classes: Learn to craft your own cocktails during weekend workshops.
  • Themed Nights: From 1920s Gatsby parties to futuristic neon nights, there’s always something new.

Must-Try

Their Jasmine Gin Fizz, infused with locally sourced tea blossoms.

Tips for a Perfect Rooftop Celebration in Guiyang

  1. Dress for the Weather: Evenings can get breezy, so bring a light jacket.
  2. Reserve Ahead: Popular spots like The Skyline Lounge fill up quickly, especially on weekends.
  3. Explore Nearby: Many rooftop bars are close to attractions like Jiaxiu Tower or Qianling Park—plan a day-to-night itinerary.
  4. Try Local Spirits: Don’t shy away from Moutai or other Guizhou liquors; they’re a unique part of the experience.

Whether you’re toasting to a special occasion or simply soaking in Guiyang’s electric energy, these rooftop bars promise unforgettable moments under the stars. Cheers to celebrating in style!
