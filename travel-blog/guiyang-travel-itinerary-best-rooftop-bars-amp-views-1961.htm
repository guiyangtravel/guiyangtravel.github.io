Guiyang Travel Itinerary: Best Rooftop Bars & Views

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is a city where modernity meets tradition. Nestled among lush green mountains, it offers a unique blend of urban energy and natural beauty. One of the best ways to experience Guiyang’s charm is by visiting its rooftop bars, where you can sip cocktails while soaking in breathtaking views of the city skyline and surrounding landscapes. Whether you're a traveler looking for a relaxing evening or a photography enthusiast chasing golden-hour shots, this itinerary will guide you through the best rooftop bars and scenic spots in Guiyang.

Why Guiyang’s Rooftop Bars Are a Must-Visit

Guiyang’s nightlife has evolved dramatically in recent years, with rooftop bars becoming a hotspot for locals and tourists alike. These elevated venues offer more than just drinks—they provide a front-row seat to the city’s stunning sunsets, twinkling city lights, and misty mountain horizons. The combination of cool breezes, creative cocktails, and Instagram-worthy backdrops makes them an essential part of any Guiyang travel plan.

The Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to explore Guiyang’s rooftop bars is between April and October, when the weather is mild and the skies are clear. Evenings are particularly magical, as the city transitions from day to night, with the sun setting behind the rolling hills.

Top Rooftop Bars in Guiyang

1. Sky Lounge at Kempinski Hotel

Location: Guanshanhu District

Perched on the top floor of the luxurious Kempinski Hotel, Sky Lounge is the epitome of elegance. This upscale bar offers panoramic views of Guiyang’s rapidly developing skyline, including the iconic Jiaxiu Tower in the distance.

What to Try:

  • Moutai Cocktail – A local twist on classic mixology, featuring Guizhou’s famous liquor.
  • Sunset Happy Hour – Discounted drinks between 5 PM and 7 PM.

Pro Tip: Arrive early to snag a seat by the edge for unobstructed views.

2. The Rooftop at Sofitel Guiyang

Location: Nanming District

Sofitel’s rooftop bar combines French sophistication with Guizhou’s natural beauty. The open-air terrace overlooks the Nanming River, creating a serene atmosphere perfect for unwinding.

Highlights:

  • Live Jazz Nights – Every Friday and Saturday evening.
  • Local Craft Beers – A selection of brews from Guiyang’s emerging microbrewery scene.

3. Cloud 9 Rooftop Bar

Location: Yunyan District

For a trendier vibe, Cloud 9 is the place to be. This bar attracts a younger crowd with its vibrant décor, DJ sets, and creative cocktails. The view of Qianling Mountain adds a natural touch to the urban setting.

Must-Order:

  • Spicy Mango Margarita – A fusion of local flavors with a kick.
  • DIY Baojiu Tasting Flight – Sample different styles of the traditional Chinese spirit.

Hidden Gems: Lesser-Known Rooftop Spots

1. The Secret Garden

Location: Huaxi District

Tucked away in Huaxi, this intimate rooftop bar feels like a hidden oasis. Surrounded by potted plants and fairy lights, it’s a romantic escape from the city’s hustle.

Why Visit?

  • Private Cabana Seating – Ideal for couples or small groups.
  • Herbal Infused Cocktails – Made with locally sourced botanicals.

2. Mountain Peak Lounge

Location: Near Qingyan Ancient Town

A short drive from the city center, this lounge offers unparalleled views of Qingyan’s ancient rooftops and the surrounding countryside. It’s a great stop if you’re exploring the historic town.

Beyond the Bars: Scenic Viewpoints in Guiyang

While rooftop bars provide a glamorous perspective, Guiyang also boasts natural and cultural viewpoints worth visiting.

1. Qianling Mountain Park

Hike up to the Hongfu Temple for a sweeping view of the city. The park is especially beautiful at sunrise.

2. Jiaxiu Tower at Night

This historic landmark lights up after dark, reflecting beautifully on the Nanming River. Capture the scene from a nearby café or river cruise.

3. Huaxi Wetland Park

For a peaceful retreat, head to this park where wooden walkways stretch over tranquil waters, offering stunning sunset views.

Tips for Enjoying Guiyang’s Rooftop Scene

  • Dress Comfortably – Evenings can get breezy, so bring a light jacket.
  • Reservations Recommended – Popular spots like Sky Lounge fill up quickly.
  • Explore Local Flavors – Don’t miss out on Guizhou’s famous sour-spicy snacks served at some bars.

Guiyang’s rooftop bars and scenic viewpoints offer a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Whether you're clinking glasses under the stars or admiring the city from a mountain peak, these experiences will make your trip unforgettable.
