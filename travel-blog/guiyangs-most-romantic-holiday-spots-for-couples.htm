Guiyang’s Most Romantic Holiday Spots for Couples

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Nestled among rolling green hills and veiled in a gentle mist, Guiyang is a city that whispers romance. For couples seeking a blend of natural beauty, cultural charm, and intimate moments away from the bustle of China’s megacities, the capital of Guizhou Province is a perfect, and often overlooked, sanctuary. It’s a place where ancient traditions meet a youthful, almost mystical energy, creating a backdrop that feels both adventurous and deeply serene. This isn't just a trip; it's an experience designed for two, a chance to forge memories in a landscape that feels both epic and personal.

A Tapestry of Green: Nature’s Embrace for Two

The first thing you’ll notice about Guiyang is its lush, almost overwhelming greenery. The city is a vibrant urban forest, and this natural setting provides the most exquisite canvas for romance.

Qianling Mountain Park: A Walk in the Clouds

A quintessential experience for any couple visiting Guiyang is a day spent at Qianling Mountain Park. This isn’t just a park; it’s an ecosystem. The moment you step inside, the city's noise fades, replaced by the chorus of birds and the rustling of ancient trees. For the active couple, the hike to the top is a shared journey of discovery, with winding paths leading through dense forests and past tranquil ponds. But the true magic lies at the summit. Here, you’ll be greeted by troops of mischievous macaque monkeys, a fun and playful sight. The real reward, however, is the panoramic view of Guiyang spread out below. Holding hands at the peak, with the city shrouded in a soft, ethereal haze, is a moment of pure, shared tranquility. For an extra touch of romance, visit the Hongfu Temple nestled on the mountainside. The scent of incense and the soft chanting of monks create a profoundly peaceful atmosphere, perfect for quiet reflection together.

Huaxi National Urban Wetland Park: A Watercolor Dream

If Qianling Mountain is about lofty heights, Huaxi Park is about serene waters. This stunning wetland park is a masterpiece of landscape design, where pavilions and bridges arc gracefully over mirror-still lakes. Rent a paddle boat and drift together through waterways fringed with weeping willows, their tendrils brushing the water’s surface. It’s a scene straight out of a classical Chinese painting. As the sun begins to set, the sky paints itself in hues of orange and pink, reflecting perfectly on the water. Stroll hand-in-hand along the wooden boardwalks, find a secluded bench, and simply be present with each other. The park’s scale allows for quiet corners, making it feel like you have your own private slice of paradise.

Cultural Whispers and Ancient Alleys

Romance isn’t just found in nature; it’s woven into the very fabric of Guiyang’s historic quarters. These spaces tell stories of the past and offer a tactile, immersive experience for couples.

Qingyan Ancient Town: Stepping Back in Time

A short drive from the city center transports you centuries back in time. Qingyan Ancient Town, with its Ming and Qing dynasty architecture, is a labyrinth of narrow, flagstone streets, towering stone gates, and hidden courtyards. Exploring these ancient alleyways together is an adventure. Discover secret gardens, peek into traditional workshops, and listen to the tales the mossy walls seem to tell. The town is also a haven for foodie couples. Share a bowl of the local specialty, Qingyan tofu, a spicy and aromatic snack sold by street vendors. The act of sharing delicious, unfamiliar food, amidst such historic charm, is a wonderfully bonding experience. Don’t miss leaving a lovelock on one of the ancient bridges or walls, a popular ritual for couples to symbolize their everlasting bond.

Jiaxiu Pavilion: The Icon of Guiyang

Standing proudly over the Nanming River, the Jiaxiu Pavilion is the iconic symbol of Guiyang. By day, its elegant, triple-eaved wooden structure is impressive. But by night, it becomes truly magical. The pavilion and its bridge are illuminated, casting a golden glow on the river below. An evening walk across the Fuyu Bridge towards the lit pavilion is incredibly romantic. The reflections on the water, the cool night air, and the sight of this historic monument make for an unforgettable stroll. It’s a prime spot for that perfect, romantic photograph to commemorate your trip.

Culinary Romance: A Feast for the Senses

No romantic getaway is complete without shared culinary experiences. Guiyang’s food scene is a fiery, flavorful, and exciting journey that is best experienced as a duo.

Siwa Wa Ma La Tang: A Spicy Adventure for Two

Dive into the local food culture at a bustling Siwa Wa (a phonetic translation for a type of hot pot) restaurant. This is not a quiet, white-tablecloth dinner; it’s a vibrant, interactive, and fun culinary adventure. You’ll choose your own skewers of vegetables, meats, and tofu, which are then cooked in a communal pot of a rich, spicy, and numbing broth (a hallmark of Guizhou’s mala flavor). The experience of cooking, sharing, and navigating the spicy flavors together is playful and engaging. It’s about laughing, trying new things, and enjoying the vibrant energy of a local hotspot. For couples, it breaks the routine and creates a memorable, sensory-filled date night.

Tea Houses and Street Food Nights

For a more subdued evening, seek out one of Guiyang’s many traditional tea houses. Sitting together and sipping on locally grown tea, like Guizhou Maojian, is a ritual of calm and connection. It’s a chance to talk, unwind, and soak in a slower pace of life. Alternatively, embark on a street food crawl through markets like Erqi Road Snack Street. The vibrant chaos, the sizzling woks, and the incredible array of smells are exhilarating. Share small plates of Chang Wang Mian (intestine noodles), Lu Dou Gao (green bean pudding), and other local delicacies. Exploring a city through its food is one of the most intimate ways to understand its heart and soul.

Beyond the City: Day Trips into the Extraordinary

Guiyang’s surroundings are home to some of China’s most spectacular natural wonders, offering unparalleled opportunities for awe-inspiring day trips.

Huangguoshu Waterfall: The Roar of Nature

A visit to Huangguoshu Waterfall, one of the largest waterfalls in Asia, is a must for any couple. The sheer power and majesty of the water crashing down into the Rhinoceros Pool is a humbling and breathtaking sight. Walk hand-in-hand through the Water-Curtain Cave, a natural tunnel behind the thundering curtain of water. Feeling the mist on your faces and hearing the incredible roar is a powerful, shared sensory experience that symbolizes the raw, untamed force of nature—and perhaps, of love itself. The surrounding park is also beautiful for a peaceful walk, far from the crowds.

Zhenyuan Ancient Town: A Riverside Retreat

For a different ancient town experience, venture to Zhenyuan. Built along the winding Wuyang River, its beauty is defined by water. The traditional stilted houses, colorful lanterns, and majestic temples are mirrored perfectly in the river’s calm surface. Take a couples’ boat ride along the river, especially in the evening when the town lights up. The serene glide through the karst landscape, with the silhouettes of temples and mountains against the twilight sky, is arguably one of the most romantic experiences in all of Guizhou. It’s peaceful, picturesque, and feels a world away from everything else.

Modern Comforts and Cozy Stays

After days of exploration, returning to a comfortable and romantic accommodation is key. Guiyang offers a range of options, from luxury international hotels with stunning city views to boutique hotels and charming guesthouses (kezhan) nestled in the hutong alleys. Choosing a place with a character that suits your relationship—be it ultra-modern or traditionally cozy—adds that final touch to your romantic holiday. Waking up to a misty mountain view from your window is the perfect start to another day of adventure for two. The city’s evolving infrastructure, including its modern metro system, makes navigating these experiences smooth and effortless, leaving you more time to focus on each other.
