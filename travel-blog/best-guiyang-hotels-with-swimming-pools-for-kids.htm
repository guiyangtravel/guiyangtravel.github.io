Best Guiyang Hotels with Swimming Pools for Kids

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

The summer heat in Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou province, can be surprisingly intense, a humid blanket that settles over the city's lush, green hills. While the city is famed as the "Forest City" for its cool summers relative to the rest of southern China, when you're traveling with children, any degree of heat calls for a refreshing escape. For families, the perfect antidote to a day of exploring ancient Qingyan Old Town or marveling at the Huangguoshu Waterfall isn't just a comfortable bed—it's a splash-filled, laughter-filled hotel swimming pool.

The quest for the perfect family-friendly hotel in Guiyang is no longer just about a central location or a kids' club; it's about finding an aquatic oasis where your little ones can burn off energy and create core vacation memories. A hotel with a dedicated kids' pool or a fantastic main pool with shallow areas is no longer a luxury; it's a necessity for a harmonious family trip. This guide dives deep into the best Guiyang hotels that understand this, offering not just a place to stay, but a watery wonderland for your children.

Why a Pool is Your Secret Weapon for a Guiyang Family Vacation

Guiyang is a treasure trove of cultural and natural wonders, but its attractions often involve a fair amount of walking and navigating crowds. A hotel pool becomes your strategic retreat.

The Ultimate Reward and Reset

After a morning spent hiking the paths around the magnificent Huangguoshu Waterfall, Asia's largest waterfall, nothing motivates a tired child more than the promise of an afternoon swim. The pool becomes a bribe, a reward, and a reset button all in one. It washes away the grumpiness and fatigue, replacing it with exhilaration and joy. For parents, it's a chance to relax by the water's edge with a cool drink while the kids are happily and safely occupied.

Embracing the Local "Shuicheng" Culture

Guiyang's nickname, "Lincheng Zhizhou, Shancheng Shuicheng" (a city built in forests, a city of mountains and waters), hints at its deep connection with water. While you're experiencing the grand scale of this at the waterfalls and rivers, the hotel pool offers a personal, intimate way for your children to connect with this elemental theme. Their splashing and playing is a modern, miniature celebration of the region's liquid heart.

A Universal Language of Fun

Travel can sometimes be overwhelming for kids, with unfamiliar food, language, and routines. The swimming pool is a universal zone of fun. It’s a place where they can instantly make friends with other young travelers from around the world without needing to speak the same language. The shared joy of cannonballs and Marco Polo transcends all barriers.

Top Guiyang Hotels with Fantastic Pools for Your Little Swimmers

Here are some of the top hotels in Guiyang that go above and beyond to cater to families with their pool facilities.

1. The Guiyang Kempinski Hotel

Nestled in the bustling Guanshanhu District, the Kempinski is a beacon of European luxury with a strong family-friendly heart. Their swimming facility is a highlight, featuring a stunning indoor pool that is a year-round haven.

  • The Pool for Kids: While there isn't a separate, standalone kids' pool, the main indoor pool includes a very generous shallow end that is perfect for toddlers and younger children to paddle and play safely. The water temperature is consistently warm, and the ambient lighting creates a serene, cave-like atmosphere that kids find magical.
  • Family Perks: The hotel often provides complimentary floaties and swimming aids for children. Poolside, you'll find ample loungers for parents to supervise comfortably. After your swim, you can retreat to spacious family rooms or suites, and the multiple on-site dining options mean even picky eaters will find something they love.
  • The Vibe: Elegant and calm. It’s the perfect place to unwind after a day in the city.

2. Guiyang Howard Johnson Johnson Hotel

A trusted international brand, this Howard Johnson property understands the needs of traveling families intimately. It’s a favorite for both business and leisure travelers seeking comfort and convenience.

  • The Pool for Kids: This hotel boasts a beautiful outdoor pool that is a massive hit during the warm summer months. The pool area is surrounded by greenery, giving it a resort-like feel right in the city. It features a distinct, separated shallow wading area specifically designed for young children, allowing them to splash independently while parents relax close by in the main pool.
  • Family Perks: The family-friendly ethos extends beyond the pool. You can often find kids eating for free promotions at the hotel's restaurants. The rooms are configured to accommodate families of four comfortably, and the service staff are notably attentive to younger guests.
  • The Vibe: Relaxed, cheerful, and unpretentionally fun. The outdoor setting makes it feel like a true vacation.

3. Crowne Plaza Guiyang Downtown

Location is key, and the Crowne Plaza sits in a prime spot for exploring Guiyang. But its real gem for families is the indoor swimming pool that focuses on both recreation and relaxation.

  • The Pool for Kids: The pool here is another excellent indoor option, ideal for Guiyang's occasional rainy days. It is spacious, clean, and well-maintained, with a significant portion of it being shallow enough for children to stand in. The large windows allow for plenty of natural light, making it a bright and inviting space for a morning or afternoon swim session.
  • Family Perks: As an IHG property, it often has great packages for families. The pool area is complemented by a well-equipped fitness center for parents and sometimes even a connected Jacuzzi (under strict parental supervision, of course). The central location means you're never far from your next adventure.
  • The Vibe: Modern, efficient, and reliably comfortable. A solid choice for the pragmatic yet fun-seeking family.

Beyond the Splash: Making the Most of Your Guiyang Family Trip

A great hotel pool is the centerpiece of your stay, but Guiyang has so much more to offer. Here’s how to build the perfect itinerary around your aquatic home base.

Pairing Pool Time with Cultural Excursions

Balance your days. Spend a morning immersed in the history of Qingyan Ancient Town, walking the cobbled streets and sampling local snacks like "Changwang Mian" (Changwang Noodles). The cultural stimulation and walking can be intense for kids, so returning to the hotel for a swim in the afternoon provides the perfect physical and mental break. The contrast between ancient stone walls and the cool, blue pool water creates a rich and varied experience.

The Mighty Huangguoshu Waterfall Day Trip

A trip to Guiyang isn't complete without witnessing the thunderous power of Huangguoshu. This is a full-day excursion that involves a lot of walking. Plan this for a day when you can return to the hotel with enough energy and time for a relaxing evening swim. The pool will be a welcome relief for little legs that have hiked the Water-Curtain Cave trail. Talk about the waterfall's power at breakfast, experience it firsthand during the day, and then let the kids recreate their own miniature versions in the hotel pool later.

Embracing the "Sour and Spicy" Food Scene

Guiyang is a foodie paradise, famous for its "suan tang yu" (sour soup fish) and countless other delicacies. A day of culinary adventure, trying new and sometimes challenging flavors for young palates, can be balanced with the familiar fun of the pool. It's a known comfort in a world of unknown tastes. Many hotels, recognizing this, also offer excellent kid-friendly buffets with international classics alongside milder local options.

Essential Tips for a Splash-Tastic Stay

To ensure your pool time is nothing but fun, keep these tips in mind when booking and packing.

  • Check the Season: While indoor pools are open year-round, always confirm the operational status of outdoor pools, as they may close during the cooler winter months (November to March).
  • Pack Smart: Don't forget swim diapers for non-toilet-trained toddlers, which are sometimes provided but not always. Goggles, swim caps (often required in Chinese pools for hygiene), and a favorite inflatable toy can make all the difference.
  • Safety First: Always, always supervise your children, even in shallow water. Familiarize yourself with the pool's depth markers and the location of life-saving equipment.
  • Timing is Everything: To avoid crowds, aim for a late morning or early afternoon swim when other guests might be out sightseeing. The pool is often quietest right after the breakfast rush.

Finding the right hotel with a fantastic pool can transform your family trip to Guiyang from a simple vacation into an unforgettable adventure. It’s the place where memories are made, where laughter echoes off the water, and where everyone, from the smallest toddler to the wisest parent, can find their own version of perfect relaxation. So pack those swimsuits, and get ready to dive into the heart of Guizhou, one happy splash at a time.
