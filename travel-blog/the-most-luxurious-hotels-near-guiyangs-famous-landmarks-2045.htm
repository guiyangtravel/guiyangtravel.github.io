The Most Luxurious Hotels Near Guiyang’s Famous Landmarks

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is a vibrant city known for its stunning natural landscapes, rich ethnic culture, and rapidly growing tourism scene. Whether you’re visiting the iconic Jiaxiu Pavilion, exploring the mystical Qianling Mountain Park, or indulging in the local Miao and Dong minority cuisines, staying in a luxurious hotel can elevate your travel experience. Here’s a curated list of the most opulent accommodations near Guiyang’s famous landmarks, ensuring comfort, style, and convenience.

1. Near Jiaxiu Pavilion: The Epitome of Elegance

Guiyang Kempinski Hotel

Just a short drive from the historic Jiaxiu Pavilion, the Guiyang Kempinski Hotel offers a seamless blend of modern sophistication and traditional Chinese aesthetics.

  • Luxury Highlights:

    • Spacious suites with panoramic city views.
    • A world-class spa featuring Guizhou-inspired treatments.
    • Fine dining at the on-site Cantonese and international restaurants.

  • Why Stay Here?
    The Kempinski’s prime location allows guests to easily explore Jiaxiu Pavilion, a symbol of Guiyang, while enjoying five-star amenities.

Sheraton Guiyang Hotel

Another top-tier option near Jiaxiu Pavilion, the Sheraton Guiyang Hotel combines convenience with lavish comfort.

  • Luxury Highlights:
    • Rooftop infinity pool overlooking the city skyline.
    • Exclusive Club Lounge access for premium guests.
    • Proximity to Nanming River for evening strolls.

2. Qianling Mountain Park: Nature Meets Luxury

Guiyang Marriott Hotel

Nestled near the serene Qianling Mountain Park, the Guiyang Marriott Hotel is perfect for travelers seeking tranquility without sacrificing luxury.

  • Luxury Highlights:

    • Private balconies with mountain or park views.
    • A gourmet breakfast buffet featuring local delicacies.
    • Direct access to hiking trails in Qianling Mountain.

  • Why Stay Here?
    After a day of exploring the park’s macaque monkeys and Buddhist temples, unwind in the hotel’s heated indoor pool.

Pullman Guiyang Lavender

For a boutique luxury experience near Qianling Mountain, the Pullman Guiyang Lavender stands out with its chic design and personalized service.

  • Luxury Highlights:
    • Lavender-themed wellness programs.
    • Rooftop bar with craft cocktails.
    • Eco-friendly amenities aligned with Guiyang’s green initiatives.

3. Qingyan Ancient Town: A Step Back in Time

The Luxe Manor Qingyan

Located just outside the UNESCO-listed Qingyan Ancient Town, The Luxe Manor offers a unique blend of historical charm and contemporary luxury.

  • Luxury Highlights:

    • Courtyard-style villas with Ming Dynasty-inspired décor.
    • Private butler service for a tailored experience.
    • On-site tea house serving rare Guizhou teas.

  • Why Stay Here?
    The hotel’s proximity to Qingyan’s cobblestone streets and ancient architecture makes it ideal for history enthusiasts.

Banyan Tree Guiyang

Though slightly farther from Qingyan, the Banyan Tree Guiyang is worth the short drive for its unparalleled privacy and exclusivity.

  • Luxury Highlights:
    • Villas with private hot spring pools.
    • Farm-to-table dining featuring organic local produce.
    • Guided cultural tours to nearby Miao villages.

4. Hongfeng Lake: Lakeside Serenity

InterContinental Guiyang Resort

Overlooking the picturesque Hongfeng Lake, the InterContinental Guiyang Resort is a haven for relaxation and outdoor activities.

  • Luxury Highlights:

    • Lakeside villas with private docks.
    • Water sports equipment rentals, including kayaks and paddleboards.
    • Sunset cruises on the lake for guests.

  • Why Stay Here?
    The resort’s tranquil setting is perfect for those looking to escape the city’s hustle while still being close to Guiyang’s attractions.

Sofitel Guiyang Hunter

Another lakeside gem, the Sofitel Guiyang Hunter combines French elegance with Guizhou’s natural beauty.

  • Luxury Highlights:
    • Michelin-starred dining experiences.
    • Infinity pool blending into the lake’s horizon.
    • Helicopter tours for a bird’s-eye view of Hongfeng Lake.

5. Guiyang’s Financial District: Urban Sophistication

Four Seasons Hotel Guiyang

Situated in the heart of Guiyang’s financial hub, the Four Seasons Hotel Guiyang caters to business and leisure travelers alike.

  • Luxury Highlights:

    • State-of-the-art meeting facilities.
    • Sky lobby with panoramic city views.
    • Signature Four Seasons spa with Himalayan salt therapies.

  • Why Stay Here?
    Its central location provides easy access to shopping malls, gourmet restaurants, and Guiyang’s nightlife.

Ritz-Carlton Guiyang

For those who demand nothing but the best, the Ritz-Carlton Guiyang delivers unmatched opulence.

  • Luxury Highlights:
    • 24-karat gold-plated bathroom fixtures.
    • Personalized shopping concierge for high-end retail therapy.
    • Exclusive whiskey bar with rare vintages.

Final Thoughts

From historic landmarks to natural wonders, Guiyang’s luxury hotels offer more than just a place to sleep—they provide an immersive experience that complements the city’s unique charm. Whether you prefer a lakeside retreat or an urban sanctuary, these accommodations ensure your stay in Guiyang is nothing short of extraordinary.
