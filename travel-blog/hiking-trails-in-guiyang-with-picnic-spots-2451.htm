Hiking Trails in Guiyang with Picnic Spots

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is a hidden gem for outdoor enthusiasts. Nestled among lush mountains and crisscrossed by crystal-clear rivers, the city offers some of the most scenic hiking trails in China. What makes these trails even more special are the picturesque picnic spots along the way—perfect for relaxing with local snacks while soaking in the breathtaking views. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a casual nature lover, here’s your ultimate guide to the best hiking trails in Guiyang with idyllic picnic spots.

Qianling Mountain Park: The Urban Oasis

Trail Overview

Qianling Mountain Park is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Located just a short distance from downtown Guiyang, this park combines easy-to-moderate trails with rich biodiversity. The main trail loops around the mountain, offering panoramic views of the city skyline.

Picnic Spot: Hongfu Temple Area

Near the iconic Hongfu Temple, you’ll find shaded groves and open grassy areas ideal for picnicking. Pack some la rou (spicy cured pork) or siwawa (a local veggie wrap) from nearby vendors and enjoy a meal surrounded by ancient trees and the soothing sounds of temple bells.

Pro Tip

Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds and catch the mist rolling over the hills—a magical sight!

Huaxi National Urban Wetland Park: A Lakeside Retreat

Trail Highlights

Huaxi Park is a sprawling wetland area with well-maintained wooden boardwalks and dirt trails. The park’s centerpiece is the serene Huaxi Lake, where you can spot migratory birds and blooming lotuses in summer.

Picnic Spot: Lakeside Meadows

The grassy meadows near the lake are perfect for spreading out a blanket. Grab some yangrou fen (spicy lamb noodles) or zhenjiang sticky rice cakes from street stalls before heading in. The gentle lapping of water and occasional bird calls make this spot incredibly peaceful.

Seasonal Bonus

Visit in autumn when the golden ginkgo trees create a stunning backdrop for your picnic.

Baihua Lake: The Hidden Paradise

Trail Adventure

Baihua Lake, about 30 minutes from downtown Guiyang, is a lesser-known treasure. The trail here winds through dense forests and opens up to stunning lake vistas. The route is moderately challenging, with some steep sections, but the views are worth every step.

Picnic Spot: Pine Forest Clearing

About halfway along the trail, you’ll stumble upon a secluded pine forest clearing. This spot is ideal for a quiet picnic. Bring some liangfen (cold mung bean jelly) and grilled fish (a local specialty) for a refreshing break.

Insider’s Note

Weekdays are the best time to visit if you prefer solitude.

Nanjiang Grand Canyon: For the Thrill-Seekers

Trail Challenge

If you’re up for an adrenaline rush, the Nanjiang Grand Canyon trail is a must. This rugged path takes you through dramatic cliffs, waterfalls, and emerald-green pools. The hike is strenuous, so come prepared with sturdy shoes and plenty of water.

Picnic Spot: Waterfall Viewpoint

After a steep climb, reward yourself with a picnic at the main waterfall viewpoint. The mist from the falls keeps the area cool, making it a refreshing stop. Pack energy-boosting snacks like nuts and dried fruits, or try suan tang (sour soup) from nearby villages for a local flavor.

Safety First

Avoid this trail during heavy rains, as the paths can get slippery.

Tips for a Perfect Hiking & Picnic Experience in Guiyang

  • Pack Light but Smart: Bring reusable utensils, a portable mat, and eco-friendly containers.
  • Local Flavors: Don’t miss trying Guizhou’s famous chili dips and herbal teas—they add a unique twist to your picnic.
  • Weather Watch: Guiyang’s weather can be unpredictable; carry a light rain jacket just in case.
  • Leave No Trace: Always clean up after your picnic to preserve these beautiful spots.

Guiyang’s hiking trails offer more than just exercise—they’re gateways to unforgettable moments in nature. Whether you’re picnicking by a tranquil lake or atop a misty mountain, the city’s landscapes promise adventure and serenity in equal measure. So lace up your boots, pack your basket, and hit the trails!
