Guiyang’s Best Hotels for Families Who Want a Central Location

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is a vibrant city known for its lush landscapes, rich ethnic culture, and mouthwatering local cuisine. For families visiting this dynamic destination, staying in a centrally located hotel can make all the difference. A well-placed hotel ensures easy access to top attractions, dining, and transportation, allowing you to maximize your time exploring.

In this guide, we’ll highlight the best family-friendly hotels in Guiyang’s city center, focusing on amenities, nearby attractions, and what makes each one stand out. Whether you’re looking for luxury, affordability, or kid-friendly perks, we’ve got you covered.

Top Picks for Family Stays in Guiyang

1. Kempinski Hotel Guiyang

Why Families Love It

Located in the heart of Guiyang’s business district, Kempinski Hotel Guiyang offers a perfect blend of luxury and convenience. The spacious family rooms provide extra comfort, and the hotel’s indoor pool is a hit with kids.

Nearby Attractions

  • Qianling Park: A short drive away, this park features a zoo, hiking trails, and a serene lake—perfect for a family day out.
  • Jiaxiu Tower: A historic landmark just 15 minutes away, offering stunning views of the Nanming River.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Complimentary cribs and baby amenities
  • Children’s menu at the on-site restaurant
  • Play area with supervised activities

2. Sofitel Guiyang Hunter

Why Families Love It

Sofitel Guiyang Hunter combines French elegance with local charm. The hotel’s family suites include separate sleeping areas for parents and kids, ensuring everyone gets a good night’s rest.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang Olympic Sports Center: Ideal for active families who enjoy sports and outdoor events.
  • Huaxi Night Market: A food lover’s paradise where kids can try local snacks like siwawa (a spicy rice wrap).

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Welcome gifts for young guests
  • Board games and movies available upon request
  • Babysitting services (advance booking required)

3. Crowne Plaza Guiyang Downtown

Why Families Love It

Crowne Plaza is a reliable choice for families seeking comfort and accessibility. The hotel’s central location makes it easy to explore Guiyang’s top sights without long commutes.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang Forest Wildlife Park: A must-visit for animal-loving kids.
  • Hongfu Temple: A peaceful retreat with beautiful gardens.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Kids eat free program (with paying adults)
  • Family-friendly room service options
  • Complimentary shuttle service to major attractions

Budget-Friendly Options for Families

4. Holiday Inn Express Guiyang City Center

Why Families Love It

For those watching their budget, Holiday Inn Express offers clean, modern rooms and a complimentary breakfast—a big plus for families.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang People’s Square: A great spot for an evening stroll.
  • Guiyang Science and Technology Museum: Interactive exhibits that kids will love.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Free stay for children under 12
  • High chairs available in the dining area
  • Early check-in options (subject to availability)

5. Vienna Hotel Guiyang Railway Station

Why Families Love It

Conveniently located near the railway station, this hotel is ideal for families arriving by train. The rooms are cozy, and the staff is exceptionally welcoming to young travelers.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang North Railway Station Plaza: A bustling area with shops and eateries.
  • Qianlingshan Park: A short taxi ride away, offering scenic walks and playgrounds.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Discounted family room rates
  • Luggage storage for hassle-free early check-ins
  • Nearby convenience stores for snacks and essentials

Luxury Retreats for Discerning Families

6. Sheraton Guiyang Hotel

Why Families Love It

Sheraton’s reputation for excellence shines here, with spacious suites, a kids’ club, and a stunning rooftop pool overlooking the city.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang Grand Theater: Catch a cultural performance or family-friendly show.
  • Wenchang Pavilion: A historic site with beautiful architecture.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Dedicated children’s concierge
  • In-room gaming consoles (available upon request)
  • Special family packages with guided tours

7. InterContinental Guiyang

Why Families Love It

For families who want the best of the best, InterContinental delivers with impeccable service, a world-class spa, and gourmet dining options.

Nearby Attractions

  • Guiyang Botanical Garden: A lush escape with themed gardens.
  • Guiyang Polar Ocean World: An underwater adventure kids won’t forget.

Kid-Friendly Perks

  • Personalized welcome amenities for children
  • Family cooking classes featuring local cuisine
  • Private transfers available for stress-free travel

Tips for Choosing the Right Family Hotel in Guiyang

1. Prioritize Location

Staying near major attractions or public transport hubs saves time and energy, especially with little ones in tow.

2. Check for Family Amenities

Look for hotels offering cribs, kid’s clubs, or babysitting services to make your stay smoother.

3. Read Reviews from Other Families

Platforms like TripAdvisor often highlight how well a hotel caters to children.

4. Consider Dining Options

Hotels with kid-friendly menus or breakfast buffets can simplify meal planning.

5. Look for Special Deals

Many hotels offer family packages that include discounts on rooms, meals, or local tours.

Guiyang is a fantastic destination for families, and choosing the right hotel can elevate your trip from good to unforgettable. Whether you prefer luxury, affordability, or something in between, these centrally located options ensure you’re never far from the action. Happy travels!
