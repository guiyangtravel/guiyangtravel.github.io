Best Budget Hotels in Guiyang for Families & Couples

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is a hidden gem in Southwest China. Known for its lush landscapes, ethnic diversity, and mouthwatering cuisine, it’s a fantastic destination for both families and couples. While luxury resorts are always an option, budget-friendly hotels can offer comfort, convenience, and local charm without breaking the bank. Whether you're exploring the city’s vibrant night markets or venturing into the surrounding karst mountains, staying at the right hotel can make all the difference.

Here’s a curated list of the best budget hotels in Guiyang that cater to families and couples, ensuring a memorable stay without overspending.

Top Budget Hotels in Guiyang for Families

Traveling with kids requires extra space, safety, and amenities. These hotels provide family-friendly accommodations at affordable prices.

1. GreenTree Inn Guiyang Railway Station Hotel

Why Families Love It:
- Proximity to Transportation: Located near Guiyang Railway Station, it’s perfect for families arriving by train.
- Spacious Rooms: Family rooms come with extra beds and kid-friendly amenities.
- Free Breakfast: A buffet-style breakfast keeps everyone fueled for the day.

Nearby Attractions:
- Jiaxiu Pavilion (10-minute drive)
- Qianling Park (15-minute drive)

2. Hanting Express Guiyang Zhongshan Road

Why Families Love It:
- Central Location: Close to shopping districts and kid-friendly attractions.
- Clean & Modern: Simple yet comfortable rooms with essential amenities.
- Affordable Rates: Great value for families on a budget.

Nearby Attractions:
- Guiyang Children’s Park (5-minute walk)
- Huaxi Wetland Park (20-minute drive)

3. 7 Days Inn Guiyang Hongfu Plaza

Why Families Love It:
- Budget-Friendly: One of the most economical options in the city.
- Convenient Access: Near public transport and family-friendly eateries.
- 24/7 Front Desk: Helpful staff for any unexpected needs.

Nearby Attractions:
- Guiyang Polar Ocean World (15-minute drive)
- Baihua Lake (30-minute drive)

Best Budget Hotels in Guiyang for Couples

Couples looking for a romantic yet affordable getaway will appreciate these cozy and well-located hotels.

1. Atour Hotel Guiyang Downtown

Why Couples Love It:
- Stylish Design: Minimalist yet chic interiors perfect for a romantic stay.
- Great Views: Some rooms offer stunning cityscapes.
- Quiet Atmosphere: Ideal for couples seeking privacy.

Nearby Attractions:
- Qingyan Ancient Town (25-minute drive)
- Nanjiang Grand Canyon (1.5-hour drive)

2. Vienna Hotel Guiyang Jinyang

Why Couples Love It:
- Elegant Rooms: European-inspired decor adds a touch of romance.
- Great Dining Options: On-site restaurant serving local and Western dishes.
- Affordable Luxury: Feels upscale without the high price tag.

Nearby Attractions:
- Guiyang Botanical Garden (10-minute drive)
- Tianhe Tan Scenic Area (40-minute drive)

3. Ibis Styles Guiyang Huaxi

Why Couples Love It:
- Vibrant Ambiance: Colorful, artsy design creates a fun vibe.
- Great Location: Close to Huaxi’s scenic spots.
- Complimentary Wi-Fi: Stay connected while exploring.

Nearby Attractions:
- Huaxi National Urban Wetland Park (5-minute walk)
- Maling River Canyon (1-hour drive)

Tips for Choosing the Best Budget Hotel in Guiyang

1. Location Matters

  • Stay near major attractions or public transport to save time and money.
  • For families, proximity to parks or kid-friendly spots is a plus.

2. Check for Family Perks

  • Look for hotels offering free stays for children under a certain age.
  • Ensure rooms have cribs or extra beds if needed.

3. Read Recent Reviews

  • Recent guest feedback can reveal cleanliness, service quality, and hidden fees.

4. Consider Local Eateries Nearby

  • Save on meals by choosing hotels near affordable local restaurants.

5. Book in Advance

  • Popular budget hotels fill up quickly, especially during peak travel seasons.

Hidden Gems: Unique Budget Stays in Guiyang

For travelers who want something different, these lesser-known budget hotels offer character and charm.

1. Guiyang Loft Youth Hostel

  • Ideal For: Young couples or small families who enjoy a social atmosphere.
  • Perks: Shared kitchen, rooftop terrace, and cultural events.

2. Miao Village Homestay

  • Ideal For: Couples wanting an authentic ethnic experience.
  • Perks: Traditional Miao-style rooms and home-cooked meals.

3. Zen Tea House Inn

  • Ideal For: Couples seeking tranquility.
  • Perks: Tea ceremonies, garden views, and a peaceful retreat from the city.

Guiyang’s budget hotels prove that affordability doesn’t mean sacrificing comfort or convenience. Whether you're traveling with family or enjoying a romantic escape, these options ensure a fantastic stay without straining your wallet. Happy travels!
