Best Airport Hotels in Guiyang for Business Meetings

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province, is rapidly emerging as a key business hub in Southwest China. With its growing economy, stunning natural landscapes, and improved infrastructure, the city attracts corporate travelers from around the world. For those visiting for business meetings, staying at an airport hotel can save time and reduce stress. Here’s a curated list of the best airport hotels in Guiyang that combine convenience, luxury, and top-notch meeting facilities.

Why Choose an Airport Hotel for Business in Guiyang?

Business travelers often prioritize efficiency, and staying near Guiyang Longdongbao International Airport (KWE) ensures quick access to flights while minimizing commute times. Many of these hotels offer:

  • Proximity to the airport (some even provide shuttle services)
  • State-of-the-art meeting rooms with high-speed internet
  • Business centers for printing, scanning, and secretarial services
  • Luxurious accommodations to unwind after a busy day

Now, let’s dive into the top picks.

Top 5 Airport Hotels in Guiyang for Business Meetings

1. Guiyang Airport Hotel (贵阳机场酒店)

Located just a 5-minute walk from the terminal, this hotel is perfect for last-minute meetings or early departures.

Key Features:

  • Meeting Rooms: Multiple conference halls with capacities ranging from 20 to 200 guests.
  • Business Services: Translation assistance, video conferencing, and IT support.
  • Dining Options: A full-service restaurant serving local and international cuisine.
  • Wellness: A fitness center and spa for relaxation.

Why Stay Here?

If you need to host a large corporate event or require seamless airport access, this hotel is a no-brainer.

2. Pullman Guiyang Airport

A premium choice for executives, the Pullman offers sophistication and efficiency.

Key Features:

  • Executive Lounge: Ideal for private discussions or informal networking.
  • Tech-Ready Meeting Spaces: Equipped with projectors, sound systems, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
  • Gourmet Dining: An on-site restaurant specializing in Cantonese and Western dishes.
  • Comfortable Rooms: Soundproofed for uninterrupted rest.

Why Stay Here?

The Pullman brand is synonymous with business travel excellence, making it a reliable option for high-profile meetings.

3. Hilton Guiyang Airport Hotel

Hilton’s reputation for luxury and service extends to its Guiyang airport property.

Key Features:

  • Flexible Event Spaces: Ballrooms and breakout rooms for different meeting formats.
  • 24/7 Business Center: For those working across time zones.
  • Airport Shuttle: Complimentary transfers to and from KWE.
  • Recreational Facilities: Indoor pool and gym to recharge.

Why Stay Here?

Hilton Honors members can earn and redeem points, adding value for frequent travelers.

4. Novotel Guiyang Airport

A blend of affordability and functionality, Novotel caters to mid-range business travelers.

Key Features:

  • Modular Meeting Rooms: Customizable setups for workshops or presentations.
  • Co-Working Spaces: For informal brainstorming sessions.
  • Eco-Friendly Initiatives: Sustainable practices for corporate social responsibility (CSR)-minded companies.
  • Nearby Attractions: Close to Qingyan Ancient Town for post-meeting exploration.

Why Stay Here?

Budget-conscious teams will appreciate the balance of cost and quality.

5. Mövenpick Hotel Guiyang Airport

Known for its Swiss hospitality, Mövenpick delivers a refined experience.

Key Features:

  • Chocolate Hour: A unique perk for networking or rewarding attendees.
  • Executive Floor: Exclusive check-in and concierge services.
  • High-Tech AV Equipment: For seamless hybrid meetings.
  • Local Cuisine: Authentic Guizhou dishes at the in-house restaurant.

Why Stay Here?

The brand’s attention to detail ensures a stress-free business trip.

Tips for Booking a Business-Friendly Airport Hotel in Guiyang

  1. Check Proximity to the Airport – Some hotels claim to be "near" but require a 15+ minute drive.
  2. Verify Meeting Room Capacities – Ensure the space fits your group size.
  3. Look for Package Deals – Many hotels offer discounted rates for bulk bookings.
  4. Read Reviews – Past guests often highlight noise levels or Wi-Fi reliability.
  5. Explore Local Transport – Even if you’re near the airport, you might need to visit downtown offices.

Beyond Business: Exploring Guiyang

While work is the priority, Guiyang’s cultural and natural attractions are worth exploring:

  • Qianling Mountain Park – A serene escape for morning jogs or meditation.
  • Jiaxiu Pavilion – A historic landmark perfect for client dinners.
  • Huaxi Night Market – Sample local delicacies like siwawa (丝娃娃) or sour fish soup (酸汤鱼).

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right airport hotel in Guiyang can make or break your business trip. Whether you prioritize luxury, convenience, or affordability, the options above cater to every need. Book early, communicate your requirements, and enjoy a productive stay in this dynamic city.
