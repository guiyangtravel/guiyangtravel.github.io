Best Airport Hotels in Guiyang for All Travel Needs

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou province, has rapidly emerged as one of China’s most intriguing travel destinations. Known for its stunning karst landscapes, rich ethnic Miao and Dong cultures, and mouthwatering cuisine—think sour fish soup and Changwang noodles—this city is a gateway to some of the country's most breathtaking natural wonders. With Longdongbao International Airport (KWE) serving as a major hub for exploring the region, finding the right airport hotel can make or break your travel experience. Whether you're a luxury seeker, a budget backpacker, a business traveler, or a family on vacation, Guiyang offers a range of airport hotels designed to meet every need. Here’s a curated guide to the best airport hotels in Guiyang for all types of travelers.

Why Stay Near Guiyang Longdongbao International Airport?

Guiyang is not just a stopover; it’s a destination brimming with adventures. From the iconic Huangguoshu Waterfall (one of Asia’s largest) to the ancient Qingyan Town and the cultural richness of Qianling Mountain Park, the city attracts tourists year-round. Moreover, Guiyang has become a hotspot for eco-tourism and digital nomads, thanks to its cool climate, green initiatives, and growing tech scene. The airport is approximately 11 kilometers from the city center, making airport hotels a strategic choice for early flights, late arrivals, or multi-day layovers. With the Guiyang Metro Line 2 connecting the airport to downtown, these hotels offer both convenience and accessibility, allowing you to maximize your time exploring without the hassle of long commutes.

Top Picks: Best Guiyang Airport Hotels by Traveler Type

1. For the Luxury Traveler: Guiyang Pullman Airport Hotel

Just a 5-minute drive from the terminal, the Guiyang Pullman Airport Hotel redefines luxury for discerning travelers. With a modern design infused with local Dong ethnic elements, the hotel offers a serene retreat after a long flight. Guests can enjoy spacious rooms with soundproof windows, premium bedding, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. The hotel features an indoor heated pool, a fully-equipped fitness center, and a spa offering traditional Chinese massages. Dining options include a sophisticated international buffet at their all-day restaurant and a curated selection of Guizhou baijiu (a local spirit) at the bar. For those looking to explore, the hotel provides a complimentary shuttle service to the airport and nearby attractions like the Guiyang Olympic Sports Center.

2. For Business Travelers: Sheraton Guiyang Airport Hotel

Convenience meets productivity at the Sheraton Guiyang Airport Hotel, connected directly to the airport via a covered walkway. This hotel is tailored for business travelers, with soundproof rooms featuring ergonomic workstations and high-speed Wi-Fi. The Club Lounge offers a quiet space for meetings or casual networking, while the 24-hour business center handles printing and secretarial services. After a busy day, guests can unwind at the heated pool or enjoy a cocktail at the rooftop bar overlooking the runway. The hotel’s strategic location is perfect for those attending events at the Guiyang International Conference Center or exploring the city’s burgeoning tech parks.

3. For Budget-Conscious Travelers: Guiyang Cloud Airport Capsule Hotel

A trendy and affordable option, the Guiyang Cloud Airport Capsule Hotel is ideal for solo travelers or backpackers looking for a clean, efficient place to rest. Located inside the airport’s terminal, this capsule hotel offers pod-style accommodations with privacy curtains, charging ports, and shared bathroom facilities. It’s perfect for those with early-morning flights or long layovers who want to avoid the hassle of transportation. The hotel also features a common area where guests can socialize, plan their trips to Fanjing Mountain or other UNESCO sites, and enjoy light refreshments. For under $20 a night, it’s a steal for no-frills comfort.

4. For Families: Holiday Inn Express Guiyang Airport

Families traveling with children will appreciate the Holiday Inn Express Guiyang Airport for its spacious family rooms and kid-friendly amenities. The hotel offers interconnected rooms, cribs upon request, and a play area to keep little ones entertained. A complimentary breakfast buffet includes both Western and Chinese options, ensuring even picky eaters are satisfied. The free shuttle service makes it easy to visit family attractions like the Guiyang Forest Wildlife Park or the city’s science museum. With a 24-hour front desk and helpful staff who speak English, parents can relax knowing their needs are covered.

5. For Extended Stays: Somerset Guiyang Airport

For travelers planning a longer visit, whether for work or leisure, Somerset Guiyang Airport provides apartment-style living with hotel comforts. Each unit includes a kitchenette, laundry facilities, and a separate living area, making it feel like a home away from home. The hotel offers a gym, a children’s playground, and regular social events for guests to mingle. Its location near the airport and major highways makes it a great base for exploring Guizhou’s countryside, including the iconic rice terraces of Jiabang or the ethnic villages of Southeastern Guizhou.

Beyond the Hotel: Exploring Guiyang’s Hotspots

Staying at an airport hotel doesn’t mean missing out on Guiyang’s vibrancy. Here are some must-visit spots easily accessible from your lodging:

Gourmet Adventures: Guiyang’s Night Markets

No trip to Guiyang is complete without indulging in its street food. Head to the Zhonghua Road Night Market or the Erqi Road Snack Street to sample local delicacies like Siwawa (a spicy vegetable wrap), grilled fish with sour soup, and crispy potato cakes. Many airport hotels can arrange food tours or provide transportation to these culinary hubs.

Cultural and Natural Wonders

Take a day trip to the Huangguoshu Waterfall, a majestic 77.8-meter cascade that inspired ancient poets. Alternatively, explore the Qianling Mountain Park to see wild monkeys and historic temples. For culture enthusiasts, the Guizhou Provincial Museum offers insights into the region’s ethnic diversity, including Miao silver craftsmanship and Dong drum towers.

Tips for Choosing the Right Airport Hotel in Guiyang

When selecting your hotel, consider the following: - Proximity vs. Amenities: If you’re only staying overnight, a terminal hotel like the capsule might suffice. For longer stays, prioritize comfort and services. - Transport Links: Check if the hotel offers free shuttles to the airport or metro stations. Guiyang’s metro is efficient and can whisk you to downtown in under 30 minutes. - Local Partnerships: Some hotels partner with tour agencies to offer discounted trips to attractions like the Dragon Palace Cave or Tianhe Lake. - Language Support: While English is spoken at major hotels, having a translation app handy can help with more remote explorations.

Guiyang’s airport hotels are more than just convenient stopovers; they are gateways to an unforgettable journey through one of China’s most culturally and naturally rich regions. Whether you’re here for business, adventure, or relaxation, there’s a perfect hotel waiting to welcome you to the heart of Guizhou.
