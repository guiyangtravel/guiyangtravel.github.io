Guiyang’s KTV & Karaoke Spots: Nightlife Travel Guide

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Guiyang, the vibrant capital of Guizhou Province, is not just about breathtaking landscapes and spicy cuisine—it’s also a hidden gem for nightlife enthusiasts. If you’re looking to belt out your favorite tunes or dance the night away, the city’s KTV and karaoke scene won’t disappoint. From luxury private rooms to budget-friendly spots, here’s your ultimate guide to Guiyang’s best karaoke experiences.

The Karaoke Culture in Guiyang

Karaoke, or KTV as it’s commonly known in China, is more than just singing—it’s a social ritual. In Guiyang, locals and tourists alike gather in private rooms equipped with high-tech sound systems, disco lights, and even themed decor. Whether it’s a birthday bash, a corporate outing, or just a fun night with friends, KTV is a must-try experience.

Why Guiyang’s KTV Stands Out

Unlike the crowded bars of bigger cities, Guiyang’s KTV spots offer a cozier, more personalized vibe. Many venues blend modern aesthetics with local Miao and Dong ethnic influences, creating a unique atmosphere. Plus, the affordability here means you can enjoy premium services without breaking the bank.

Top KTV Spots in Guiyang

1. Millionaire Party KTV

Located in the heart of the city, Millionaire Party is the go-to spot for luxury karaoke. Think VIP rooms with leather couches, 4K screens, and professional-grade microphones. Their drink menu is extensive, featuring everything from craft cocktails to premium baijiu.

Highlights:
- Themed rooms (Hollywood, Star Wars, etc.)
- 24/7 service for late-night sing-alongs
- English song selections available

2. Happy Melody KTV

A favorite among locals, Happy Melody offers a balance of quality and affordability. The sound system is top-notch, and the staff is known for their friendly service. Don’t miss their “all-you-can-sing” weekday promotions.

Highlights:
- Budget-friendly packages
- Great for group outings
- Snack bar with local Guizhou bites

3. Star Light Karaoke

For those who love a futuristic vibe, Star Light delivers with neon-lit rooms and holographic stage effects. It’s a hit with younger crowds and often hosts karaoke competitions.

Highlights:
- High-energy ambiance
- Social media-worthy decor
- Live DJ on weekends

What to Expect at a Guiyang KTV

Room Types and Pricing

Most KTVs offer rooms in tiers:
- Small rooms (4-6 people): Ideal for intimate gatherings.
- Medium rooms (8-12 people): Great for birthday parties.
- VIP suites (15+ people): Equipped with dance floors and karaoke stages.

Prices vary, but expect to pay around ¥200-¥800 per hour depending on the venue and time (weekends are pricier).

Food and Drinks

Many KTVs serve more than just drinks—think hotpot, skewers, and even full-course meals. Happy Melody, for instance, offers a spicy Guizhou-style hotpot right in your room!

Etiquette Tips

  • Reservations: Book ahead, especially on weekends.
  • Song selection: Most systems have Mandarin, Cantonese, and English options.
  • Tipping: Not mandatory, but appreciated for exceptional service.

Beyond Karaoke: Guiyang’s Nightlife Hotspots

While KTV is a blast, Guiyang’s nightlife doesn’t stop there. Pair your singing session with these nearby attractions:

1. Bar Street (Qianling Xi Lu)

A hub of trendy bars and live music venues. Try “Drunk Cat” for craft beers or “Moon Club” for electronic beats.

2. Night Markets

Head to Erqi Road Night Market for post-KTV snacks like grilled fish or stinky tofu.

3. Late-Night Spas

Many 24-hour spas, like “Dragonfly Relaxation,” offer foot massages—perfect after a long night of singing.

Final Tips for the Ultimate KTV Experience

  • Go with a group: The more, the merrier!
  • Warm up your voice: Try local throat-soothing teas like “pang da hai” before hitting high notes.
  • Dress comfortably: Some venues get warm under the disco lights.

Whether you’re a karaoke pro or a first-timer, Guiyang’s KTV scene promises unforgettable nights filled with music, laughter, and maybe a few off-key moments. So grab the mic, and let the good times roll!
